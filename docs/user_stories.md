# User Stories: CFO Office

These stories represent the core requirements for the AI-powered Cashflow Analysis app, focused on the needs of the CFO and their senior leadership team.

## 1. The CFO (Strategic Liquidity)
*   **Goal:** Instant, consolidated global visibility.
*   **Story:** "As the CFO, I want to ask, 'What is our total spendable cash across all global entities in USD right now?' and get a single number that accounts for bank balances, pending gateway payouts, and current tax liabilities."

## 2. VP of Finance (Performance & Leakage)
*   **Goal:** Identifying operational inefficiencies and revenue gaps.
*   **Story:** "As the VP of Finance, I want to ask Gemini to 'Analyze why our ad revenue collection in the APAC region is 10 days slower than last quarter' and receive a list of specific agencies and clear remediation steps to close the gap."

## 3. Treasury Manager (Cash Optimization)
*   **Goal:** Yield optimization and inter-company movement.
*   **Story:** "As the Treasury Manager, I want to ask, 'Which subsidiaries have idle cash exceeding $1M that isn't in a high-yield account?' and have Gemini propose a sweep schedule that minimizes transfer fees."

## 4. FP&A Lead (Content ROI & Burn)
*   **Goal:** Correlating financial outflows with content performance.
*   **Story:** "As the FP&A Lead, I want to ask, 'Compare the cash burn of our 'Tentpole Thrillers' vs. 'Mid-budget Dramas' over the last 6 months' and see if our investment velocity matches our subscriber acquisition targets."

## 5. Accounts Receivable/Payable Manager (Working Capital)
*   **Goal:** Transactional efficiency.
*   **Story:** "As the AR/AP Manager, I want Gemini to 'Find all vendors offering early payment discounts we missed last month' and automatically calculate the liquidity cost of those missed opportunities."
